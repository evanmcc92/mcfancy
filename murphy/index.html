Murphy Bed

This installment features how to create usable space while maintaining the multi-functionality of a room.

This Murphy Bed has been constructed with a built-in, foldaway utility tabletop.

The carcass and internal bed frame are constructed from solid and ply oak.

The lifting mechanism is based on a kit of pressurized pistons and customized hardware pivots.
